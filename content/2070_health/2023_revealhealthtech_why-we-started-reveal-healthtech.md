# Why we started Reveal HealthTech

*Reveal HealthTech blog — 2023*

> Body not retrievable in full via web fetch. Summary below is reconstructed from search snippets. See URL for the original.

## Founding context

Reveal HealthTech was founded after a meeting where the founder learned about the technology challenges that **portfolio companies in the U.S.** were facing — particularly in the healthcare sector, which had remained underserved by traditional engineering outsourcing.

## Three structural challenges identified

### 1. Talent

Healthcare firms struggled to onboard engineering talent — **75% of engineering roles were open for more than 3 months** at the time of founding.

### 2. Cultural misalignment

Expectation mismatches among employees led to **high attrition in software engineering teams**.

### 3. Lack of domain expertise

Outsourcing partners lacked **domain understanding of healthcare** — its complex terminology, regulatory requirements, multi-stakeholder interactions, and data-privacy considerations (HIPAA, etc.).

## Reveal's positioning

Reveal HealthTech offers **technology and strategy outsourcing for global healthcare systems and startups**, anchored in deep healthcare domain expertise. It is one of the three companies that 2070 Health incubated in its first nine months (alongside Elevate Now and Nivaan Care).
