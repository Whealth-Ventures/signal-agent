# BeatO Raises INR 42 Cr Funding From W Health Ventures, PharmEasy

*Inc42 — July 7, 2021*

> Story reconstructed from Inc42, PR Newswire, YourStory, and Healthcare IT Today coverage; the web fetch did not return the full body in this pass.

## Key points

- **BeatO** — India's leading digital care ecosystem for **chronic-condition management** (anchored on diabetes) — raised **USD 5.7M (~INR 42 Cr)** in funding **led by W Health Ventures**.
- **Co-investors**: e-pharmacy unicorn **PharmEasy** (Threpsi Solutions Pvt Ltd), **Merisis VP**, plus existing investors **Orios VP** and **Leo Capital**.
- This was BeatO's **pre-Series B** round; with the round, BeatO had raised **>USD 10M over the trailing 12 months** across Series A and pre-Series B.
- **Use of funds**:
  1. Growth in subscriber base across multiple channels and geographies.
  2. Product expansion into **other cardiometabolic conditions** beyond diabetes.
- **Traction at announcement**:
  - 500,000 app installs.
  - 300,000 paid members (adding 25,000/month).
  - Revenues up 600% in the period.
  - Annual gross revenue run-rate of **~USD 10M**.

## Why this matters for the W Health Ventures corpus

BeatO is one of the most material **Fund I** investments — chronic disease at scale, India-for-India. BeatO went on to raise a $33M Series B from Lightrock in 2022.
