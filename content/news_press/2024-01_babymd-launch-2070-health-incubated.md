# BabyMD launch — 2070 Health's omnichannel pediatric venture

*Composite reconstruction — January 2024*

> No single canonical launch press release was reachable via web fetch. Summary below is composited from Peak XV (Spark), 2070 Health's own news index, BabyMD coverage in Adgully / MediaInfoline / Prodwrks, and Tracxn.

## Key points

- **BabyMD** — India's first omnichannel pediatric healthcare brand — was launched in **January 2024**.
- **Founders**: **Deeksha Senguttuvan** and **Hrishikesh Thite**.
- **HQ**: Bengaluru.
- **Incubated by**: **2070 Health**.
- **Backed by**: **W Health Ventures** and **Spark (Peak XV)**.
- **Footprint**: Five clinics across Bengaluru — **Electronic City, Hosa Road, HSR Layout, Varthur, and Bellandur**.
- **Services**:
  - Outpatient pediatric consultations.
  - Vaccinations.
  - Lactation support.
  - Developmental assessments and therapies.
  - 24x7 access to pediatricians via **chat, video, and in-clinic** consults.
- Notable campaign: **#BooTheFlu** — India's first joyride-based flu vaccination initiative (2024-2025).

## Why this matters for the W Health Ventures / 2070 Health corpus

BabyMD is the **fourth founding 2070 Health portfolio company** (after Elevate Now, Nivaan Care, and Reveal HealthTech). It's the cleanest expression of the venture studio's "omnichannel + single-specialty" thesis — vertical pediatrics, phygital footprint, deep clinical depth. Its Peak XV Spark / W Health Ventures co-backing is the first time a 2070 Health spin-out brought in an external Tier-1 VC at incubation.
