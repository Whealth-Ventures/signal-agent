# W Health Ventures announces initial close of Fund II at Rs 550Cr to launch and scale healthcare companies

*BioSpectrum India — February 2026*

> Content not retrievable in full via web fetch. Summary below is reconstructed from search snippets and concurrent Inc42 / DealStreetAsia / Entrepreneur India coverage. See URL for the original.

## Key points

- W Health Ventures has marked the **first close** of its second fund at **₹550 Cr (~$60.6 Mn)**, against a target corpus of ₹600–630 Cr.
- The fund will back **8–10 early-stage healthtech startups** over the next four years, with average cheques of ₹30–50 Cr.
- Pankaj Jethwani, Managing Partner: Fund II is "a company creation fund." Translation: 2070 Health (the venture studio) identifies key problem statements, designs solutions, and builds companies to address those gaps.
- Stated focus areas: **oncology** (Everhope), **psychiatry**, **longevity & modern preventive health**, **geriatrics**, **chronic pain management**.
- Fund II's first deployment was **Everhope Oncology** (March 2025) — $10M co-investment with Narayana Health to launch a national oncology platform with centers initially in Delhi NCR and Mumbai.
