## Key points

- **Everhope Oncology** — a new joint venture from Narayana Health, W Health Ventures, and 2070 Health, announced with **$10 million in seed funding**.
- Initial centers in **Delhi NCR and Mumbai**, with plans to expand to **10 cities over three years**.
- Comprehensive cancer care services: diagnosis, chemotherapy, radiation, surgery, and palliative care.
- Positioning: a modern, comfortable, infection-free environment with personalized attention, minimal wait times, and improved patient experience.
- This is W Health Ventures Fund II's first disclosed investment ("day zero" deployment).
