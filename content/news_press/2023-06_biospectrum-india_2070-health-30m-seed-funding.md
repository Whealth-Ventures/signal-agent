# India's first healthcare venture studio 2070 Health raises $30 M in seed funding

*BioSpectrum India — June 2023*

> Content not retrievable in full via web fetch. Summary below is reconstructed from search snippets. See URL for the original.

## Key points

- **2070 Health**, India's first Healthcare Venture Studio, has raised **$30 million** from W Health Ventures.
- Funds will be used to:
  1. Build the 2070 Health platform.
  2. Seed newly launched portfolio companies.
- 2070 Health has launched **three companies** in the past nine months: **ElevateNow**, **Nivaan Care**, and **Reveal HealthTech**.
- Mission: create a unique platform that builds healthcare companies from scratch to make high-quality healthcare more accessible and affordable in India.
