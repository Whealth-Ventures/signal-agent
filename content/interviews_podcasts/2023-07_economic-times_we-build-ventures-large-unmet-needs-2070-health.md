# We build ventures in spaces with large unmet needs: 2070 Health

*Economic Times — July 2023*

> Content not retrievable via web fetch — articleshow.cms URLs are heavily JavaScript-driven. Summary below is reconstructed from concurrent coverage and the firm's stated thesis. See URL for the original.

## Key points

- The thesis-defining quote captured in the headline: "We build ventures in spaces with large unmet needs."
- 2070 Health takes a **bottom-up problem-first approach**: identify a problem space with large unmet need, develop a solution thesis, then match the idea with a founder.
- Founded in October 2022 by Pankaj Jethwani (CEO) and Sunil Wadhwani (Chairman) with the vision to create India's first healthcare-focused venture studio.
- Backed with $30M seed funding from W Health Ventures (announced June 2023).
- At the time of the interview, the studio had launched **three** companies — Elevate Now (medical obesity management), Nivaan Care (pain management), and Reveal HealthTech (tech-and-strategy outsourcing for healthcare).
- The studio commits between $1M and $3M per incubation and provides extensive operational and strategic support until each company achieves PMF.
