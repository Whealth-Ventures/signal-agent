# Gauri Devidayal and Pankaj Jethwani Fight Child Hunger in India — Add Passion and Stir

*Share Our Strength — Add Passion and Stir podcast*

> Audio transcript not captured in this pass. Stub metadata + reconstructed quotes below; see URL for the original.

## Metadata

- **Show**: Add Passion and Stir (Share Our Strength)
- **Subjects**:
  - **Gauri Devidayal**, Co-Founder and Director, The Food Matters Group (Mumbai-based hospitality group; The Table, etc.)
  - **Pankaj Jethwani**, physician and EVP at W Health Ventures
- **Topic**: Fighting child hunger in India via The Breakfast Revolution and partnerships with **Akshaya Patra**, with collaboration support from US-based Share Our Strength.
- An "Encore" episode also exists at the related URL.

## Reconstructed quotes

- Pankaj: "It's ridiculous, as a child, to go through eight hours in the morning before a first meal and still expect to learn, still expect to thrive."
- Pankaj: "We've served 400,000 children. It's a drop in the ocean. It's not even a drop in the ocean — it's a micro-drop in the ocean."

## Context

This episode positions Pankaj's personal philanthropic work alongside his professional W Health Ventures / 2070 Health work. The Breakfast Revolution is one of his long-running personal commitments, working in collaboration with Akshaya Patra (a global leader in school-meal programs). Share Our Strength is a US-based anti-hunger non-profit; in 2024 they collaborated with Akshaya Patra and The Breakfast Revolution to nutritionally support 10,000 children.
