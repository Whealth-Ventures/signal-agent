## Show-notes summary (reconstructed)

- The Desi VC is an award-winning podcast featuring conversations with top investors and founders on trends, insights, and personal stories in the **India–US startup ecosystem**.
- Pankaj's career arc: started as a **Primary Care Physician in Mumbai**, where he saw firsthand the gaps in healthcare delivery; joined **The Boston Consulting Group** in India to help shape BCG's work with government agencies on healthcare-at-scale; then moved to the US for **Iora Health** and adjacent operating roles before returning to India healthcare investing.
- The episode covers W Health's investment thesis at the time — omnichannel, vertically integrated, tech-forward Indian healthcare delivery models, plus India-US-corridor B2B services.
