# Why we invested in Elevate Now

*W Health Ventures blog — March 2023 (republished by Vaibhav Singhal on LinkedIn the same week)*

> The whealthventures.com blog page is JavaScript-rendered and didn't surface body text via web fetch. Summary below is reconstructed from search snippets. See URL for the original.

## Investment rationale

### 1. Huge unsolved problem

The weight-loss market in India is **~$12B annually**. **95% of users** end up looking for a sustainable solution that doesn't lead to weight rebound — and almost all current solutions either don't work, don't last, or carry meaningful safety risk.

### 2. Omnichannel distribution from day zero

Elevate Now adopted an **omnichannel distribution strategy from day zero** — starting with a **co-branded offline clinic in Tier 2 India** and planning to scale to **10+ clinics across India within a year**. This is consistent with W Health Ventures' broader thesis that durable Indian healthtech requires both digital reach and physical anchor points.

### 3. Founders' track record

The founding team has experience scaling Indian tech brands from their prior work at **Ola and Swiggy** — combined expertise in consumer businesses and building scalable backend operations in India. Healthcare delivery + consumer-tech operating muscle is a rare combination.
