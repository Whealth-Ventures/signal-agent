# Mumbai-based 2070 Health is looking to build the next Ola and Swiggy of healthtech

*YourStory — August 2023*

## Key points

- 2070 Health is a venture studio launched in 2022 by Pankaj Jethwani (CEO) and Sunil Wadhwani (Chairman) — a platform to create new healthtech companies from scratch.
- The thesis: "There is still no healthtech solution that is as ubiquitous as what an Ola, Swiggy, or Flipkart is to Indian consumer-tech," per Pankaj.
- The studio takes a bottom-up approach: it researches a problem area first, then matches the idea with a founder who can execute it.
- Investment scale: 2070 Health commits between $1 million and $3 million per incubation.
- At time of article, three companies had been incubated:
  - **Elevate Now** — India's first medical obesity management platform.
  - **Nivaan Care** — India's first end-to-end pain management brand.
  - **Reveal HealthTech** — Technology and strategy outsourcing for global healthcare systems and startups.
- The studio raised $30 million in seed funding from W Health Ventures in June 2023 to build the platform and seed newly launched companies.
- 2070 Health's startups are positioned as either "Indian startups building in India for India" (Elevate Now, Nivaan Care) or "building in India for the world" (Reveal HealthTech).
