# Venture studio 2070 Health raises $30M to seed healthtech companies

*YourStory — June 2023*

> Content not retrievable in full via web fetch. Summary below is reconstructed from search snippets and concurrent coverage. See URL for the original.

## Key points

- 2070 Health, India's first healthcare venture studio, raised **$30 million** in seed funding from W Health Ventures.
- Founded in 2022 by Pankaj Jethwani (CEO) and Sunil Wadhwani (Chairman).
- Funds will be used to build the 2070 Health platform and seed newly launched companies.
- At the time of announcement, 2070 Health had launched three companies in the prior nine months: **ElevateNow** (medical obesity management), **Nivaan Care** (end-to-end pain management), and **Reveal HealthTech** (technology and strategy outsourcing for global healthcare systems and startups).
- Pankaj's framing: 2070 Health is building healthcare companies focused on patient outcomes, not consumer-grade healthtech apps.
