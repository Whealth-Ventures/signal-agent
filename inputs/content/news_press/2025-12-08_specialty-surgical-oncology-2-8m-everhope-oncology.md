# Specialty Surgical Oncology secures $2.8M from Everhope Oncology to scale

*eHealth Magazine / startupnews.fyi / Daily Excelsior — December 8, 2025*

## Key points

- **Specialty Surgical Oncology Hospital (SSO)** secured **USD 2.8 million** in funding from the **Everhope Oncology platform** on December 8, 2025.
- Everhope Oncology is the joint venture between **Narayana Health, W Health Ventures, and 2070 Health** that launched in March 2025 with $10M in seed funding.

## About SSO

- A Mumbai-based **chain of surgical-cancer hospitals** in Western India.
- Founded in **2021** by a team of **7 cancer surgeons** led by **Dr. Sanket Mehta**, a Tata-trained cancer surgeon.
- Mission: address critical gaps in **cancer surgery** in India.

## About Everhope Oncology

- A diversified network of specialized patient-centric cancer facilities offering surgical and medical oncology, aiming to redefine the perception of cancer care in India.
- Joint venture between Narayana Health and W Health Ventures (via 2070 Health). First investment from W Health Ventures' Fund II.

## Use of capital

- Launch of **three new centers in Mumbai**.
- Expansion across **Western India** — including Mumbai and Ahmedabad — and major cities in **Maharashtra and Gujarat**.

## Why this matters for the W Health Ventures / 2070 Health corpus

This is the first publicly disclosed **platform follow-on investment** by Everhope Oncology since its launch — confirming the original "platform plus M&A" thesis (Everhope as the umbrella platform absorbing high-quality regional cancer-care providers, rather than only operating greenfield centers). It also extends the W Health Ventures + 2070 Health corpus into late 2025 / early 2026.
