## Key points

- Narayana Health, W Health Ventures, and 2070 Health jointly launched **Everhope Oncology** to improve cancer care in India.
- **$10 million seed funding** to establish medical and surgical oncology facilities in Delhi NCR and Mumbai, with plans to expand to **10 cities over the next three years**.
