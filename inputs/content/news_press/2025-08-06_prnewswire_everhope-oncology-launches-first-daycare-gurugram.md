# Everhope Oncology Launches First Cancer Daycare Centre in Gurugram

*PR Newswire (PTI) — August 6, 2025*

## Key points

- **Launch date**: August 6, 2025.
- **Location**: Sector 65, Gurugram.
- **Capacity**: 15-bed dedicated cancer daycare centre — Everhope's **first physical facility**.
- **Backers**: Narayana Health, W Health Ventures, and 2070 Health.

## Operating model

Everhope Oncology is positioned as **India's first patient-first, technology-enabled cancer daycare platform**. Each centre is designed to offer the following under one roof:
- Same-day **chemotherapy** and **immunotherapy**.
- **Diagnostics**.
- **Nutrition** support.
- **Mental health counselling**.
- **Concierge** support.

## Differentiators (per company framing)

- **Zero wait times**.
- **Expert-led tumor boards**.
- **Integrated care pathways**.
- A patient-experience design intended to reduce the logistical, financial, and emotional burdens typically associated with cancer treatment.

## Capital and roadmap context

- Everhope Oncology raised **$10M in seed funding** at launch (March 2025) — one of the largest early-stage healthcare investments in India that year.
- After Gurugram, Everhope plans to open additional centres across **NCR** (South Delhi, Noida) in the coming months.
- The brand is finalising locations in **Mumbai and Bangalore**.
- Total roadmap: **10-city expansion** over three years.

## Why this matters

This is the first publicly announced **operating-asset launch** by Everhope Oncology — six months after the founding $10M raise. It validates the underlying thesis that Everhope is a "platform of physical centers" rather than a pure digital brand.
