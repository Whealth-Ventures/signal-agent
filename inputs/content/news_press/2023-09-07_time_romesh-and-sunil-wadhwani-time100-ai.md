## Key points

- TIME named **Sunil Wadhwani** and his brother **Romesh Wadhwani** as members of its inaugural **TIME 100 AI Leaders** list (September 2023).
- The recognition is anchored in their philanthropic and AI work, including:
  - An initial **$30M commitment to Wadhwani AI** (a Mumbai-based non-profit institute focused on AI for social impact).
  - **$60M+ committed to date** across Wadhwani AI initiatives.
  - Wadhwani AI builds and deploys scalable AI solutions in **healthcare, education, and agriculture** for underserved communities, partnering with governments in the Global South.
- Sunil Wadhwani's broader portfolio includes:
  - **WISH Foundation** (Wadhwani Initiative for Sustainable Healthcare) — primary-care delivery in low-income communities; >1,000 technology-enabled health clinics serving 30M+ people.
  - **W Health Ventures** — for-profit healthcare VC firm.
  - **2070 Health** — venture studio building healthcare companies from scratch.
- Quote (from concurrent coverage): "We thought that in the U.S., China, and Europe, AI is being leveraged to help people who are already well-off, but maybe we can make India the global leader in applying AI for social good." — Sunil Wadhwani.
- Romesh Wadhwani is Chairman of **SAIGroup**, an operating group of advanced AI enterprises.
- Sunil Wadhwani co-founded **IGATE** (30,000+ employee IT services company), sold in 2015 for ~$4.5B.
