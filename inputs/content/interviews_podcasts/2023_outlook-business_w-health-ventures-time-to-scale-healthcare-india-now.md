# W Health Ventures Discusses Why Time To Scale Healthcare In India Is Now

*Outlook Business — 2023*

> Body not retrievable in full via web fetch. Summary below is reconstructed from search snippets. See URL for the original.

## Setting

W Health Ventures hosted an industry discussion in **Delhi**, with a panel of investors and operators from the Indian healthcare space. The discussion centered around the **Indian healthcare industry as a trillion-dollar opportunity by 2030**.

## Pankaj Jethwani's headline quote

> "Healthcare companies that have doubled down on **omnichannel distribution** see a **lower cost of patient acquisition**, comparable unit economics mainly due to higher AOVs (average order value), significantly **better lifetime value** given multiple patient touchpoints, and most importantly, **better clinical outcomes**."

## Main panel themes

- The need to focus on **patient experience**.
- **Collaboration with legacy players** (hospital chains, doctors, payers).
- **Omnichannel distribution** as the dominant winning model — limitations of online-only.
- The rise of **single-specialty care chains**.
- The need for **healthcare financing solutions**.
- Unanimous panel view: the **time to scale and accelerate healthcare transformation in India is now**.

## Why this matters for the W Health Ventures corpus

This panel write-up is one of the cleanest public articulations of the omnichannel + single-specialty thesis that later anchors W Health Ventures' Fund II.
